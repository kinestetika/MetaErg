In humans, a defect in the AS gene causes citrullinemia, a genetic disease characterized by severe vomiting spells and mental retardation. AS is a homotetrameric enzyme of chains of about 400 amino-acid residues. An arginine seems to be important for the enzyme's catalytic mechanism.
