31 100 100 Class II Chelatase: a family of ATP-independent monomeric or homodimeric enzymes that catalyze the insertion of metal into protoporphyrin rings. This family includes protoporphyrin IX ferrochelatase (HemH), sirohydrochlorin ferrochelatase (SirB) and the cobaltochelatases, CbiK and CbiX.
