71 100 100 RbcX protein. The RBCX protein has been identified as having a possible chaperone-like function. The rbcX gene is juxtaposed to and cotranscribed with rbcL and rbcS encoding RuBisCO in Anabaena sp. CA. RbcX has been shown to possess a chaperone-like function assisting correct folding of RuBisCO in E. coli expression studies and is needed for RuBisCO to reach its maximal activity.
