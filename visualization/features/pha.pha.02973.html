58 100 100 Queuosine biosynthesis protein. Queuosine (Q) biosynthesis protein, or S-adenosylmethionine:tRNA -ribosyltransferase-isomerase, is required for the synthesis of the queuosine precursor (oQ). It catalyzes the transfer and isomerisation of the ribose moiety from AdoMet to the 7-aminomethyl group of 7-deazaguanine (preQ1-tRNA) to form epoxyqueuosine (oQ-tRNA). Q is a hypermodified nucleoside usually found at the first position of the anticodon of asparagine, aspartate, histidine, and tyrosine tRNAs. In Streptococcus gordonii, QueA has been shown to play a role in the regulation of arginine deiminase genes.
